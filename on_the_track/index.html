Resources
  • Official F1 TV

    (Requires subscription) Users can watch F1 and adjacent circuits live along with highlights, documentaries, and mini-shows

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) Formula 1 Partnership

    Various articles providing a look into the integral data science behind F1 race strategy, car performance, and competitor analysis (There is also a link at the bottom of the page to a form for a free e-book)

  • IEEE Xplore

    (IEEE = Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers Explore) This database contains scholarly, technical content covering disciplines such as engineering, physics, and mathematics. Content can be found on the race cars, aerodynamics, data analysis and more of F1 racing. (Hint: Perform an advanced search with "Formula 1" and try specifying a date range!)

  • ScienceDirect

    Science-focused database with journals covering engineering, physics, sports science, and more. Content can be found on the science, aerodynamics, and physics behind F1 racing. (Hint: Try an advanced search with "Formula 1" and term from the Official F1 glossary! Use "AND" to separate keywords)
