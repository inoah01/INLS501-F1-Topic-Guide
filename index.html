Welcome to the PetrolHeads Public Library: F1 Collection!

This is a guide for new to moderate fans of F1 looking to learn more about the global, multi-billion dollar sport. This guide is designed for new and moderate Formula 1 (F1) fans, providing a comprehensive overview of the sport, its history, and various aspects on and off the track. This is accomplished through the curation of various websites, YouTube videos, and databases.

On this "Home" page, you will find a few resources providing a high-level overview of the sport. Explore the resources provided in the "On the Track" and "Off the Track" sections to dive deeper into the world of F1. Scroll down to get started!

About this library and collection

The PetrolHeads Public Library's mission is to provide automotive enthusiasts and non-enthusiasts alike with a centralized place for learning more about motorsports and automobiles. The F1 Collection of the PetrolHeads Public Library aims to fulfill this mission by focusing on digital resources such as websites, videos, and databases from both academic and non-academic sources, covering both high-level and low-level details of Formula 1 racing.
